Espresso Lounge at Jeju-do

After enjoying some Jeju black pork at Neulbom Heukdwaeji, we have just one more attraction to hit before driving to the East side of the island. It's an attraction welcomed by most coffee lovers but not someone like my little nugget.Located in the heart of Jeju's downtown area just across the street from Neulbom (where we had lunch), Espresso Lounge is a pilgrimage well worth the time and effort whether you love coffee or not. The two-story premise combines a coffee shop and bakery cafe both under one roof. I love a coffee shop with an abundance of natural daylight. Just by looking at the facility, you knew they're very serious about their coffee.
